Place your company font files in this directory.

Required font files based on the design system:
1. CompanySans-Regular.woff2 and CompanySans-Regular.woff
2. CompanySans-Bold.woff2 and CompanySans-Bold.woff
3. CompanySerif-Regular.woff2 and CompanySerif-Regular.woff

If you don't have these exact files, you'll need to modify the company-design-system.css file to match your actual font filenames.
