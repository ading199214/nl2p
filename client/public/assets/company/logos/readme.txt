Place your company logo files in this directory.

Recommended logo files:
1. company-logo.svg (vector format for best quality)
2. company-logo.png (raster format with transparency, at least 200px height)
3. company-logo-small.png (smaller version for mobile, 100px height)
4. company-logo-dark.png (version for dark backgrounds)

Logo files should follow your company's branding guidelines.
